Read My Book

Alexa Skill

How to get started with the skill:

1. To invoke the skill say - "Alexa,Read my book".

2. After invoking the skill you can ask Alexa to read a book by Saying "read bookname".

3. To Know the Author of the book, Say "Alexa who wrote this?".

4. To know what books are available, Say "Alexa what books can you read?".
